Last updated: June 1, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Clave mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application") created by Kaya Software AS ("Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This Application is designed as a logic puzzle and gamified cybersecurity simulation.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide core matchmaking and leaderboard functionality, the Application collects and stores specific data on our secure Cloudflare database:

  • Account Information: If you choose to link an account, we collect an authentication identifier provided by Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In.
  • Profile Data: Usernames, Rank Points, generated Vault configurations, and interaction logs (Breach History).
  • Device & Ad Data: Device identifiers and diagnostic data may be collected automatically by integrated third-party SDKs for the purpose of serving advertisements and handling in-app purchases.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party providers to facilitate advertisements, authentication, purchases, and core backend functionality. These services have their own Privacy Policies regarding data collection:

  • Google Play Services & Google Sign-In

3. Local Data Storage

Certain preferences, such as localization settings and active session tokens, are stored locally on your device's secure storage. This data remains on your device to ensure seamless authentication and user experience.

4. Children's Privacy

The Service Provider does not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. AdMob and other services may serve ads; we encourage parents to monitor their children's mobile usage. If you are aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

We value your trust in providing us with your data. We utilize commercially acceptable means of protecting it, including secure server architectures and encrypted communication protocols. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure and reliable.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
